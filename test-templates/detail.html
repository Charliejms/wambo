A Mr. Robot Reading List

I miss Mr. Robot. It won’t be back on TV until June

Player Piano

In a future where automation replaces human labor, the managers and the engineers live big while those whose jobs are replaced live in squalor. Instead of building a future for all, the elites built a future for themselves. Manager Paul meets secret society intent on ripping apart a system producing nothing but disparity.

Moral Blindness

Makes the case that evil isn’t usually a bad man intent on doing bad things, but rather our system’s train never stopping to question where it’s going. Bauman is not an easy dude to read. His mind palace is a messy chaos of books most people will never read. But the idea of moral blindness is useful and I think it’s a key theme in Mr. Robot.

Saving the Modern Soul
